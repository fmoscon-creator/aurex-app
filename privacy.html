AUREX — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

1. Information We Collect

AUREX collects the following information when you create an account:

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Phone number (optional, for alerts via WhatsApp/Telegram)
  • Portfolio data (assets, quantities, purchase prices you enter)
  • Watchlist data (lists and assets you follow)
  • Alert preferences (notification settings)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized portfolio tracking and AI signals
  • To send alerts via WhatsApp, Telegram, Email or Push notifications (when enabled)
  • To process subscription payments via Apple In-App Purchase
  • To improve our AI analysis engine

3. Data Sources

Market data comes from public APIs:

  • Cryptocurrency prices: Binance API
  • Stock/ETF/Commodity prices: Yahoo Finance
  • Macro data: FRED (Federal Reserve Economic Data)
  • Geopolitical data: GDELT Project

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (PostgreSQL) with encryption at rest and in transit. Our backend runs on Railway with HTTPS.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase (authentication and database)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)
  • Apple In-App Purchase (payment processing)

6. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade or share your personal data with third parties. Your portfolio and watchlist data is private and only accessible to you.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile tab in the app. You can also contact us at fmoscon@gmail.com to request data deletion.

8. Children's Privacy

AUREX is not intended for children under 17. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

10. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: fmoscon@gmail.com
